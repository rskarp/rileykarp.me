Why would I like to join the DALI team?

If this challenge project is any indication of what it will be like working with DALI, then I can't wait to (hopefully) join the team! I've had so much fun learning how to use HTML/CSS and designing my own webpage. I enjoy coding because I get to just press a bunch of buttons on a keyboard, and it turns into something cool! (It's like pressing all the buttons in an elevator, but it does something productive and doesn't annoy everyone around you!)

I love UI/UX design and development because I get to combine the rigidity and structure (and button-pressing aspect) of coding, with the fun, colorful, and creative part of design. Although I only have experience with a few design and programming applications (GIMP, SolidWorks, Github, and now HTML5), I'm a quick learner and I'm excited to learn more while working in a super cool environment with comfy, colorful couches and awesome team members!
